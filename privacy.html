Privacy Policy for PrispaShop_1

Effective date: 2026-04-10

What this app is

PrispaShop_1 is a private internal tool operated by PrispaShop. It connects to Pinterest only to support authorized Pinterest account management tasks such as reading boards, creating Pins, and viewing limited account-related data that the account owner has approved. It is not a public consumer app.

What data we access

We may access and store technical account data necessary to operate the app, including OAuth tokens, board identifiers, Pin identifiers, and basic analytics or reporting data returned by Pinterest. We do not sell personal data. We do not share Pinterest account data with third parties.

How data is used

Data is used only for operating the app, troubleshooting, and improving internal workflows. Access is limited to the authorized operator of the tool.

Contact

To request data deletion or ask about data access, contact: owner@prispashop.com

Updates

We may update this policy by posting a revised version on this page.
